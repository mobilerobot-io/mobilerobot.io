Onboard Communication Over an I2C Bus

On vehical computation and communication systems can become complex rather quickly, with that, we can leverage I2C for close proximity communication amoung our Macro and Micro Controllers.

For reasons documentented in Micro Communicaiton Options and RPI Vs. Arduion we established the reasons why our vehicle will host a couple arduino’s and a Raspberry Pi.

Macro to Micro Controller Communication

Our basic architecture has the Raspberry Pi controlling show on the Vehical itself. The Arduino’s are used to collect sensor inputs then send them to the macro-controller for further processing and storage.
