Logic Level Converters

I bought a couple LLC from Amazon from Hiletgo. 4 Channel, bidirectional Logic Level Controller.

  sdlc lv1 --  -- hv1 sclk
  sclk lv2 --  -- hv2 sdlc
  3.3v  lv --  -- hv  5v
       gnd --  -- gnd
  sclk lv3 --  -- hv3 sdlc
  sdlc lv4 --  -- hv4 sclk

The left sides is low voltage.

  • low - moderate bandwidth (400k simplex)
  • super simple and cheap
  • need two wires for data and clock signals (yes, you also need power and mostlikely a ground) so 4 wires …
  • it has been around forever and very well established sets hardware
  • very low latency(?) Great for realtime up to X delay
  • low bandwidth
